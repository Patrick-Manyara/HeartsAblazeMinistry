The Focus of Hearts Ablaze Ministry

The inspiration to this work is drawn from the Gospel of Luke and Acts of the Apostles,1 Corinthians 15 who provide firm foundation, sound doctrine and ministry pattern to what God wants to accomplish through us. The key guiding verse is Luke 24:45 from which our name, mandate and experience of HEARTS ABLAZING for God in our generation is prophetically founded. Our vision is to be ablaze and see hearts blazing as they journey with God by purpose.
