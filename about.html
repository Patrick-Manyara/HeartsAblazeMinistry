The Focus of Hearts Ablaze Ministry

The inspiration to this work is drawn from the Gospel of Luke and Acts of the Apostles,1 Corinthians 15 who provide firm foundation, sound doctrine and ministry pattern to what God wants to accomplish through us. The key guiding verse is Luke 24:45 from which our name, mandate and experience of HEARTS ABLAZING for God in our generation is prophetically founded. Our vision is to be ablaze and see hearts blazing as they journey with God by purpose.

Our Name

The Name of the church shall be “Hearts Ablaze Church-International” or “-HEARTS ABLAZE CHURCH-KENYA .The church is to provide apostolic cover/shoulder to churches and ministries that identify with her grace and pattern as mandated by The Holy Spirit.

Bible Study Fellowship

The weekly bible study shall be a a parallel entity that will seek to reach out to the unchurched, those who desire to be disciple, mentored and be equipped with knowledge and experience in scripture for practical kingdom living. The membership is open to members from different denominations and so its leadership for sustainability.
